Whitewater Rafting Shearwater bend 1 day High This involves navigating through rapids and white water, which are sections of the river where the water flows fast and turbulent. Whitewater rafting can be very exciting and challenging, but also potentially dangerous. Whitewater rapids are graded from I to VI, with I being the easiest and VI being the most extreme $20
Flatwater Rafting Angels rain pass 2 days Low This type of rafting involves floating on calm water, such as lakes, reservoirs, or slow-moving rivers. Flatwater rafting is more relaxed and leisurely, and suitable for beginners and families. The great thing about this type of rafting is sight seeing, fishing, game viewing. $40
Sea Rafting Golden Waters corner 4 days Moderate This type of rafting involves navigating through the ocean, including trips to remote islands, snorkeling, and wildlife viewing. Sea rafting can be affected by tides, currents, and waves, and requires special equipment and skills. You also get to see others kayaking and sailing. $80
Paddle Boats Trip Mantaranta rapids 3 days Low Paddle boat rafting is a type of whitewater rafting where a group of people paddle down a river in an inflatable raft. The raft is usually steered by a guide who gives commands to the paddlers. Paddle boat rafting is a fun and exciting way to enjoy the outdoors and experience the thrill of the rapids. $60
Gear Boats Trip The Black Cataract 3 days Low A type of whitewater rafting where a large raft carries most of the gear and supplies for a multi-day trip. The gear boat is usually rowed by an experienced guide, while the other rafts are paddled by the guests. Gear boat rafting allows the guests to enjoy the rapids without worrying about the weight and balance of their rafts. $60
Oar & Paddle Blue Water Pools 3 days Low Combined oar and paddle rafting is a type of whitewater rafting where the guide uses oars to steer and control the raft, while the guests use paddles to help with propulsion and maneuvering. This way, the guests can enjoy some of the thrill and teamwork of paddle rafting, while the guide can provide more stability and safety with the oars. $60
