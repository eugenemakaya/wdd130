About Us

Creating rape free world.

We are a Non Governmental Organisation in Zimbabwe which provides a sensitive response for rape survivors in government hospitals. We provide comprehensive management including medical management, counselling and support services as well as prevention, awareness raising and advocacy surrounding gender based violence issues.
